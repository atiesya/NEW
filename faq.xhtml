🌷 Frequently Asked Questions 🌷

🍰 1. Are your pastries and drinks Halal?

Yes, absolutely! At Sweet Bloom Bakers, we are committed to ensuring that all our ingredients are Halal-certified. You can enjoy our treats with peace of mind.

📦 2. How can I place an order or make a purchase?

Visit us in-store or order via Instagram DM, WhatsApp, or our order form. Pre-orders for custom items are highly recommended!

🌱 3. Do you offer vegan-friendly options?

We currently have limited vegan options, but we are working on adding more soon. Please ask our team for availability.
